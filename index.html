Joelle Abu-Hani

I completed my MSc in Mapping and Geo-Information Engineering from the Technion, under the supervision of Prof. Sagi Filin . Prior to that, I earned my BSc in Mapping and Geo-Information Engineering from the Technion.

My research lies at the intersection of 3D geometry processing, computer vision, and machine learning, and their application to the domain of 3D urban modeling. During my MSc, I focused on accurate feature extraction and normal estimation in challenging, noisy 3D point clouds. Specifically, I worked on optimizing unsupervised point cloud processing algorithms to enhance data quality by attenuating noise and revealing the underlying structure.
